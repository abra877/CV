Biography

My name is David Ghantt. I am an actor from Shawnee, Oklahoma, though I now live in Los Angeles like every other actor. I have a wide range of acting skills, from action and thriller to comedy and drama. I am known for playing charismatic male leads, and have been described as the world's most attractive man by several media outlets. Nowadays I prefer subtler roles, including those where I play a father figure, which I am to my six children. Apart from acting, I have experience as a producer for many films with my own production company Plan B Entertainment, of which I am the CEO. In my spare time, I enjoy philanthropy, architecture, and skateboarding.
